# SLH Spark · Outreach Follow-up Templates
**Purpose:** Ready-to-paste follow-up messages for users who got an initial outreach but haven't responded. Matched to segments in `ops/OUTREACH_BATCH_*.md`.

**Channel:** Telegram personal from `@osifeu_prog` (NOT bot).

**When to use:**
- **T+24h silent:** soft nudge, different angle
- **T+3d silent:** different ask (simpler), or check-in
- **T+1w silent:** close the loop — "sounds like not a fit right now, keeping you in the loop"

Do NOT send all 3 on autopilot. Use judgment: if they opened the link but didn't reply → 24h follow-up. If nothing at all → maybe skip to 1w check-in and move on.

## Global rule: 3 strikes, then stop

- If someone hasn't responded after T+24h, T+3d, AND T+1w → move to `ops/archived_contacts_<date>.md` and don't contact for 30-60 days.
- Quality > quantity. A customer who responds after 3 touches is worth 10x one who never does.
- Your reputation is the bank account — don't overdraw it.
