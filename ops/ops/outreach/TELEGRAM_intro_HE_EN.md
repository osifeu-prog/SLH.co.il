## Tips

- **Open with a hook**, not the company name (e.g., "I'll skip the deck — let me show you a working hardware wallet that's heart-beating to Postgres while you read this.")
- **Personalize one line** based on their portfolio (use the Hebrew/English email customization grid)
- **End with a specific ask** (30 min call, not "let me know your thoughts")
- **Use Telegram emoji sparingly** — 1-2 per message max in formal contexts; more is fine for crypto-native VCs
- **Don't include the disclaimer in DM** — it lives on /investors.html and they'll see it. Adding it here makes the message feel defensive.
