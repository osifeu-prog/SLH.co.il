## What is Threat Intelligence?

SLH's Threat Intelligence Hub is a **decentralized fraud detection system** powered by community members like you. Every report you submit helps protect the entire network.

### How It Works

1. **You notice something suspicious** → wallet, phone, user activity
2. **You submit evidence** → what happened, when, proof
3. **Community verifies** → multiple users validate your report
4. **Bad actors marked** → flagged in the system, broadcast to all 25 bots
5. **You earn reputation** → ZVK tokens for accurate reports

## 🎯 Getting Started

### Step 1: Identify Fraud

Watch for these red flags:

**Wallet Red Flags:**
- Transaction to known scam addresses
- Sudden large withdrawals
- Transfers to sanctioned regions
- Mixing with stolen funds

**User Red Flags:**
- Asking for passwords or keys
- Promises of unrealistic returns (>100%)
- Pressure to act quickly
- Impersonating staff
- Building trust then requesting money

**Phone Red Flags:**
- Unsolicited offers
- Requesting payment upfront
- Different story than promised
- No verifiable business address
- Threatening messages

### Step 2: Gather Evidence

Before reporting, collect:

✅ **Required:**
- What type of fraud (scam, stolen funds, sanctioned, identity theft, other)
- Description of what happened
- Severity level (1-10)

✅ **Optional but helpful:**
- Screenshots/photos
- Chat logs (redact personal info)
- Transaction hashes
- Links to evidence

### Step 3: Submit Your Report

Go to **Admin Panel → Threat Intelligence → Submit Fraud Report**

Fill in:
- **Your User ID** — required for reputation tracking
- **Target Phone/Wallet** — who/what you're reporting
- **Fraud Type** — select from dropdown
- **Severity** — how serious (1=minor, 10=critical)
- **Evidence Description** — detailed story
- **Evidence URL** (optional) — screenshot/document link

Click **[ SUBMIT REPORT ]**

## ⚠️ False Positive Prevention

### Your Reputation Depends On Accuracy

**If 5+ reports are marked false:**
- Accuracy score drops
- Reputation tier decreases
- ZVK earnings reduced
- Can lose reporting privileges

### How to Avoid False Reports

1. **Verify before reporting**
   - Check wallet/phone in Arkham first
   - Look for independent confirmation
   - Ask in community channels

2. **Be specific**
   - Vague reports get marked false
   - Include dates, amounts, details
   - Link to evidence

3. **Understand context**
   - Not everyone with reports is bad
   - Legitimate traders get reported sometimes
   - Multiple perspectives matter

4. **Appeals Process**
   - Reported user can challenge
   - Need 3+ reviewers to overturn
   - Evidence must be clear

## 🆘 Getting Help

### Common Questions

**Q: What if I report someone false?**
A: First false report = warning. Fifth false = temporary ban from reporting.

**Q: Can I remain anonymous?**
A: No. Reputation is tied to your User ID for accountability.

**Q: How long until report is verified?**
A: 24-72 hours usually. Complex cases may take 1 week.

**Q: What happens to flagged users?**
A: Automated warnings on bots, reduced access to features, eventual ban at 100 ZUZ.

**Q: Can I appeal a false report on me?**
A: Yes. 3+ expert reviewers must agree it's false.
