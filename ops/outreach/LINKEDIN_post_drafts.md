## Posting Strategy

**Where:**
- LinkedIn personal (Osif's profile) — primary
- LinkedIn company page (if exists) — secondary
- Twitter/X — version C (already short)
- Crypto-native LinkedIn groups — version A or C
- Israeli tech LinkedIn groups (BiTech, IsraelTech, etc.) — version A in Hebrew (translate)

**When:**
- Tuesday 9-11 AM Israel time = best for B2B engagement
- Don't post Friday afternoon or Sunday — religious community drop-off
- Avoid Israeli holidays week

**After posting:**
- DM 5-10 connections in your network with: "Saw this — thought of you because [specific reason]. Mind sharing if it fits anyone in your network?"
- Reply to every comment within 4 hours
- Pin the post to your profile for the duration of the raise

**Track:**
- Use a UTM-tagged link: `https://slh-nft.com/investors.html?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=series_a_announce`
- Set up Google Analytics goal for "investor pageview" 

---

## DON'T

- Don't promise returns ("guaranteed 3x" → flagged immediately by anyone in fintech)
- Don't disparage competitors by name
- Don't use stock photos — the ESP32 photo of YOUR hardware is the killer image
- Don't tag random VCs without warm context (looks desperate)
- Don't post the same content 3x — write one strong post, leave it
