English Abstract

SLH Spark operates a 5-token ecosystem: SLH (BEP-20 governance/utility on BSC, 110.75M supply, contract 0xACb0A09...), MNH (TON-native stablecoin for healthcare/community payments), ZVK (contributor rewards, convertible to SLH), REP (non-transferable reputation score), and ZUZ (ILS-pegged internal accounting unit).

Four earn paths require no purchase: staking (Revenue Share Pool), referrals, contributions (ZVK), and Sudoku-to-AIC gameplay. Newcomers should start with the micro-test tier (0.01 TON or 0.0005 BNB) before scaling up. No yield guarantees - the Revenue Share Pool distributes real protocol earnings (course sales, marketplace fees, SaaS revenue) by stake weight.
