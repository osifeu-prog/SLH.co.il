Getting Started #01

Welcome to the SLH Ecosystem. Here is how to start using the platform in 3 minutes.

Open Telegram

Download Telegram from telegram.org if you do not have it. Available on iOS, Android, Desktop, and Web.

Find our main bot

Search for @SLH_AIR_bot in Telegram, or click: t.me/SLH_AIR_bot

Send /start

The bot will greet you and show your dashboard with SLH and ZVK token balances. You can explore all free features immediately.

Explore the menu

You will see buttons for: Market Prices, Investments, Wallet, Risk Management, Games, and Referrals. Try each one to understand the system.

All basic features are free. You only pay when you want to make real deposits or access premium features.

How to Find Your Telegram ID #02

Your Telegram ID is a unique number that identifies your account. You need it to log into the web dashboard.

Method 1: Using @userinfobot (Easiest)

Open @userinfobot

Search for @userinfobot in Telegram and open the chat.

Send /start

The bot will immediately reply with your ID, username, and name. Copy the number.

Method 2: Using SLH_AIR_bot

Open @SLH_AIR_bot

Send /start to the bot. Your dashboard will show your current balances.

Check the referral link

Click "Invite" in the menu. Your referral link contains your ID: t.me/SLH_AIR_bot?start=YOUR_ID

Method 3: Using Telegram Settings

On some Telegram clients, go to Settings and your ID may be shown under your profile. On Telegram Desktop, it might be visible in the API section.

Investment Guide #03

The SLH Investment House offers real cryptocurrency deposits with monthly returns.

How Deposits Work

Choose a plan

Send "Investments" in the bot menu. 4 plans available: Monthly (4%), Quarterly (4.5%), Semi-Annual (5%), Annual (5.4%).

Send TON

Transfer TON cryptocurrency to the wallet address shown. Minimum depends on plan (1-25 TON).

Send screenshot proof

Take a screenshot of your transfer confirmation and send it to the bot as a photo.

Wait for approval

An admin will verify your payment and approve it. You will get a confirmation message.

Earn monthly returns

Returns are calculated monthly and added to your account. Check with /mydeposits command.

Never invest more than you can afford to lose. Cryptocurrency investments carry risk. Past returns do not guarantee future results.

Useful Commands

/deposit - Start a new deposit

/mydeposits - View your active deposits

/statement - View your account statement

/portfolio - View your full portfolio

/withdraw - Request a withdrawal

Wallet & Token Transfers #05

The SLH ecosystem uses 4 types of tokens:

Token Types

TON - Real cryptocurrency. Used for deposits and withdrawals. Tradable on exchanges.

SLH - Ecosystem utility token on BSC blockchain. Used for premium access and governance.

ZVK - Internal reward token. Earned through games and referrals. Convertible to TON.

🛡️ ZUZ - Anti-fraud "Mark of Cain" token. Assigned by Guardian bot reports. 100 ZUZ = auto-ban across all groups. Cross-group intelligence.

How to Transfer Tokens

Internal transfer (SLH/ZVK)

Use /pay [amount] [token] [user_id] in the bot. Or use the web dashboard Transfer tab.

Blockchain transfer

Use /send in the bot for on-chain TON transfers.

ZVK to TON Conversion

10 ZVK = 1 TON | 50 ZVK = 4 TON | 100 ZVK = 7 TON

Affiliate Program (2 Tiers) #06

Two-tier affiliate program. Earn from purchases of your direct referrals and their referrals only. No deeper levels.

Commission Rates

Tier 1 (direct): 20% of their purchases

Tier 2 (their referrals): 5% of purchases

Paid from referral budget funded by real system revenue. Not from other users' deposits.

Gen 4: 2%

How to Refer

Get your referral link

In @SLH_AIR_bot, click "Invite" to get your unique link: t.me/SLH_AIR_bot?start=YOUR_ID

Share with friends

Send the link to friends, post on social media, or share in groups.

Earn automatically

When anyone in your network makes a deposit, you earn commission automatically. Check on the web dashboard.

Example: 100 TON Deposit in Your Network

If someone 3 levels deep deposits 100 TON, you earn 3 TON (3%). If it was a direct referral, you earn 10 TON (10%).

Your network grows exponentially. If each person refers just 3 people, by generation 5 you have 243 people earning you commissions!

Security Best Practices #11

Never share your private keys, PIN codes, or wallet seed phrases with anyone. SLH staff will NEVER ask for these.

Enable 2FA in Telegram settings for extra account protection.

Verify bot names - only use official @SLH_AIR_bot. Beware of fake bots with similar names.

Keep records - use /statement to regularly check your account activity.

Report suspicious activity - contact /support or call 058-420-3384.

Frequently Asked Questions #12

Q: Is this a real investment platform?

SLH is a digital investment ecosystem built on real blockchain technology. All TON deposits are real cryptocurrency transactions. However, like all investments, returns are not guaranteed.

Q: How do I withdraw my money?

Use /withdraw in @SLH_AIR_bot. Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours after admin approval.

Q: What happens if I lose my phone?

Your account is tied to your Telegram account. Log into Telegram on a new device and all your data will be there.

Q: Can I use this from outside Israel?

Yes! The platform supports Hebrew, English, Russian, Arabic, and French. Anyone with Telegram can use it.

Q: How do I contact support?

Send /support in any SLH bot, or call 058-420-3384, or email osif@spark-ind.com.

Q: What is the minimum investment?

The monthly plan starts at just 1 TON (approximately 4 ILS at current prices).
